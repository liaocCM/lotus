Write a `stringutil.Slugify(s string) string` function in the `stringutil` package that converts a string to a URL-safe slug. Rules: lowercase, spaces become hyphens, strip non-alphanumeric characters (except hyphens), collapse consecutive hyphens, trim leading/trailing hyphens. Include table-driven tests with at least 8 cases covering: unicode characters, leading/trailing spaces, consecutive special characters, empty string, already-valid slug, mixed case, numbers, and long strings.
